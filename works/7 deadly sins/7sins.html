The most brutal and powerful order of Holy Knights in the Kingdom of Liones is called Nanatsu no Taizai, or The Seven Deadly Sins. They were created by seven vicious criminals who had all been found guilty of heinous offenses and had been given the symbols of seven beasts tattooed on their bodies. They serve as the show's titular main protagonists.
